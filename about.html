About AMLX

Your partner in machine learning innovation.

AMLX is an applied machine learning company founded by entrepreneurial students from Eindhoven University of Technology with a focus on practical, high-impact systems.

Our story

From MOTEX to a broader safety systems ambition.

AMLX grew out of MOTEX, a startup developed during a master's trajectory at Eindhoven University of Technology. The team started from the belief that technology should not stay theoretical, but should solve meaningful problems in the real world.

That journey taught AMLX how to validate a challenge, work closely with stakeholders, iterate quickly, and stay focused on measurable impact. Over time, it became clear that the opportunity was bigger than a single project.

Today AMLX applies the same practical, engineering-driven way of working across safety, infrastructure, and industrial contexts where intelligent systems need to be useful in practice.
